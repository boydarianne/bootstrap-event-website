About

Art Exhibits was founded as a way to bring local and international artists, art collectors, and art lovers together! The founder of Art Exhibits, Arianne Boyd, saw the opportunity to create this space when she noticed there was an opporunity to create a more fluid and informational space that makes it easier for the art community to find each other and connect! As an artist herself she knows the importance of bringing everyone together to share in the love of art! Browse our website to find the latest events happening near you. Events will include art exhibits, live paintings, paint and sips, body painting, and more!
